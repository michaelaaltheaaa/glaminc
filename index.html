ABOUT

  Welcome to Get Your Glam On Inc, where individuality meets expertise in hair care. With over 25 years of dedicated experience, our salon is more than just a place to get your hair done – it's a sanctuary for nurturing and celebrating your natural locks.

  At Get Your Glam On Inc, we believe in hair care without compromise, prioritizing the health and integrity of your hair above all else. We guarantee comfortable hairstyles by offering only glueless installs, ensuring that styles are never too tight and meticulously washing, conditioning and properly moisturizing your hair before every style thus providing gentle, nurturing care aimed at enhancing your hair’s health and revealing its inherent vibrancy.

  We understand that hair is more than mere strands – it holds profound significance as a cultural, spiritual, and emotional emblem. It's a symbol of heritage, a conduit to our ancestors, and a reflection of our innermost selves. With a deep respect for the natural beauty of each client's hair, our salon is an oasis where you can unwind and rejuvenate while receiving the best of care.

  Embrace the beauty of your natural hair with confidence at Get Your Glam On Inc. Book your appointment today and experience the difference of expert care and genuine passion for healthy, radiant hair.
